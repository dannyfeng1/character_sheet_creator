Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition Character Creator






Welcome to the Dunegon and Dragons 5th Edition Character Creator!

This application was created to guide new players through the wonder but complicated game that is Dungeons and Dragons.

Character creation is often an overwhelming process for new players as there are so many customizable options due to the fantasy nature of D&D.

Hopefully this application will streamline character creation by providing the user with digestible information.

After the user has submitted the character creation form, a simple character sheet with random stats will be generated for you.

Keep in mind that stat distributions will vary depending on the type of character you want to play.

Ask your Dungeon Master for more guidance when you begin your first adventure!


On the character sheet will be interactable elements that will return information when clicked on!

In addition, a brief tutorial will begin that will guide the user through the basic D&D mechanics.

I hope this application will ease the transition into the wonderful role playing game, Dungeons and Dragons.

I wish everyone the best on their first campaign!
